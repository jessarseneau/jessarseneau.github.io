Untitled (with cloud), 2024

Video, sound stereo, 2:40 min.

2:44 min.





On the bank of a river, with the drama of crashing waves in the background, stands a glowing, cloud-shaped outline. Sometimes static, other times unstable, the camera seems to bear witness to its persistent and solitary presence.
