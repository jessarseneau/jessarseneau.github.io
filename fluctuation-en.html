Fluctuation

2019

Digital video. Sound stereo.

00:04:42



What are the factors forming and transforming the waves? The various waveforms are triggered by uncountable events and circumstances hat are whether internal or external to their composite, conductive to their defined formation. An invisible geography, similar to a virtual configuration, is creating a friction that triggers a fluctuation. The movement of increasing and decreasing varies according to a network of components within a system, where elements are transferred in many directions and channels. Some waves proliferate more quickly and grow faster than others, with the ability to absorb everything on their path.
