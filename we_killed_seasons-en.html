We Killed Seasons, 2026
Video, sound stereo
26:40 min.











We Killed Seasons (2026) is a video installation that imagines a world without seasons. We follow five people who inhabit a place that is familiar, and, yet it is fundamentally altered. Two of them speak to one another in loose conversation about the changing meaning of the sun, while another voice attempts to make sense of a world in which rhythm and time have come undone. The work reflects on the disappearance of natural cycles and what it means to lose the temporal structures in an environment that is becoming increasingly inhospitable.
